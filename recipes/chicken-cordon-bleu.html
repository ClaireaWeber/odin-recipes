Chicken Cordon Bleu

Description

Chicken cordon Bleu is the perfect meal for a cold day. It is one of my absolute favorites!

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 6 slices swiss cheese
  • 4 slices cooked ham
  • 1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat a 7x11-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness. Sprinkle each piece of chicken on both sides with salt and pepper. Place 1 Swiss cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of each breast.
  3. Roll up each breast and secure with toothpicks. Place in the prepared baking dish and sprinkle chicken evenly with bread crumbs.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink, 30 to 35 minutes.
  5. Remove from the oven, and place 1/2 Swiss cheese slice on top of each breast. Bake for 3 to 5 minutes more or until cheese has melted. Serve immediately.
